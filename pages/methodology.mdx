## Data updating

When dealing with current year data, the figures shown are an annual projection using the already available months.

    Example as follows: ...
    Current year, indicator: 2021, Home sales
    Available months: January (1000 home sales), February (2000 home sales)
    Projected figure: Average of available months * total months on a year = ((1000+2000/2)*12 = 18000
    Figure shown for 2021: 18.000 home sales

As a rule of thumb, the data is updated quarterly, although in some cases it may vary. In any case, the last revised date can be consulted in the header of each indicator post.
